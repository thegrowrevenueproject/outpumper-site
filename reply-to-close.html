The follow-up cadence that gets answers

  1. Day 0 — Send the reply above. One clear ask, one easy yes.
  2. Day 3 — If no answer, send the bump for that scenario, one line, no guilt.
  3. Day 7 — Add value, not pressure: one relevant proof point or resource, then re-offer the call.
  4. Day 12 — The no-guilt close: "Should I leave this here for now?" A one-word reply is easy to give, which is why it works.
  5. Stop — After four touches with no response, move on and re-approach next quarter. Past this point, persistence costs more reputation than it returns.
