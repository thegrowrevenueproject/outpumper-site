The follow-up cadence that gets answers

  1. Day 0 — Send the reply above, one clear ask, one easy yes.
  2. Day 3 — If no answer, a one-line bump: "Floating this back up, {{first}}, still worth a quick look?"
  3. Day 7 — Add value, not pressure: share one relevant proof point or resource, then re-offer the call.
  4. Day 12 — The no-guilt close: "Should I leave this here for now?" A one-word reply is easy to give, which is why it works.
  5. Stop — After four touches with no response, move on and re-approach next quarter. Persistence past this point costs more reputation than it returns.
