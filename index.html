Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need Microsoft 365?

Yes. OutPumper is an Outlook add-in. If you have desktop Outlook connected to a Microsoft 365 account, you are ready to go. That is the only requirement.

Will this get my account banned?

OutPumper is built around Microsoft's sending limits. It throttles automatically, sends in natural waves, and stops before you approach a limit, so your account stays in good standing.

How is this different from Instantly?

Instantly sends from its own servers, which means buying 30 to 50 throwaway domains, warming them for weeks, and still sending from an address your prospects have never seen. OutPumper sends from your own Outlook and your real domain, with no warmup and no extra domains.

Can I connect external providers?

Yes. Connect Google Workspace, SendGrid, Mailgun, Amazon SES, or any standard SMTP server, and manage all of it from one dashboard inside Outlook.

Is there a trial?
