Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need Microsoft 365?

Yes. OutPumper is an Outlook add-in. If you have desktop Outlook connected to a Microsoft 365 account, you are ready to go. That is the only requirement.

Will this get my account banned?

OutPumper is built around Microsoft's sending limits. It throttles automatically, sends in natural waves, and stops before you approach a limit, so your account stays in good standing.

How is this different from Instantly?

Instantly and Smartlead push you to buy 30 to 50 throwaway domains, spin up hundreds of mailboxes, and warm them for weeks, the very pattern Google, Yahoo and Microsoft now flag. OutPumper sends through a couple of real Microsoft 365 Outlook mailboxes you already trust, so you send on day one and ride Microsoft's deliverability.

Can I connect external providers?

Yes. Connect Google Workspace, SendGrid, Mailgun, Amazon SES, or any standard SMTP server, and manage all of it from one dashboard inside Outlook.

Is there a trial?

The Pro and Agency plans include a 7-day free trial. We collect a card up front but charge nothing during the trial. Cancel before day seven and you pay nothing. The Lifetime plan is a one-time purchase and does not include a trial.

What is the refund policy?

Thirty days, no questions asked. If OutPumper is not right for you, you get every cent back.
