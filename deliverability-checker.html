What we check

The four records that decide the inbox.

SPF

Tells receivers which servers may send for your domain. Missing or broken SPF is a fast track to spam.

DKIM

Cryptographically signs your mail so receivers trust it is really you. Without it, your mail looks forgeable.

DMARC

Tells receivers what to do with mail that fails SPF or DKIM. No DMARC means no policy, and weaker trust.

MX

Confirms your domain can receive mail, which receivers expect from a legitimate sender.
