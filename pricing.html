Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need Microsoft 365?

Yes. If you have desktop Outlook connected to a Microsoft 365 account, you are ready. That is the only requirement.

Will this get me banned?

OutPumper is built around Microsoft's limits, with smart throttling, automatic safety stops, and wave-pattern sending designed to keep your account clean.

How is this different from Instantly?

Instantly has you buy 30 to 50 throwaway domains, spin up hundreds of mailboxes, and warm them for weeks, the very pattern Google, Yahoo and Microsoft now flag. OutPumper sends through a couple of real Microsoft 365 Outlook mailboxes, with no domain farm and no warmup.

Can I use shared mailboxes?

Yes. OutPumper finds them automatically and rotates across them. Shared mailboxes are free in Microsoft 365, so you can scale without paying for extra accounts.

Can I connect external email providers?

Yes. Google Workspace, SendGrid, Mailgun, Amazon SES, or any standard SMTP server, all managed from one dashboard inside Outlook.

Is there a trial?

Seven days, full features, on Pro and Agency. We collect a card up front but charge nothing during the trial. Cancel before day seven and you pay nothing. The Lifetime plan is a one-time purchase with no trial.

What is the refund policy?

Thirty days, no questions asked. If OutPumper does not work for you, you get every cent back.
