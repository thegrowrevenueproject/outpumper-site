The real cost

vs Instantly

Instantly has you buy and warm dozens of throwaway domains and spin up mailbox after mailbox, the warmup footprint inbox providers increasingly flag, all run from a separate app. OutPumper sends through a couple of real Microsoft 365 Outlook mailboxes on day one, with no domain farm and nothing to warm up.

vs Smartlead

Smartlead runs the same playbook: dozens of domains, stacks of mailboxes, weeks of warmup, with your contacts and campaigns living on its platform. OutPumper keeps the sending, the data, and the reputation inside your own Microsoft 365 tenant.

vs Woodpecker

Woodpecker is solid, but it still needs external SMTP, DNS setup, and warmed-up domains. OutPumper removes that entire layer. No SMTP wiring, no domain farm, just your own Outlook and Microsoft 365.
